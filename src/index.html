Rules of WizMemory Game

    The goal of WizMemory game is to match all pairs of cards by remembering their positions on the screen. A grid of Wizcards is displayed face down on the screen. Each card has a hidden image that can only be revealed by clicking on it.

  • ⭐ Start the game by flipping over one card
  • ⭐ If the card matches, they stay face-up, and you score points
  • ⭐ If the cards do not match, they are automatically flipped back face down after a short delay and you can try again
  • ⭐ WizGame ends when all pairs have been matched
